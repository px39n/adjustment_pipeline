## Event
 
$\chi_{f}^{2}=\sum_{j=1}^{f} X_{j}^{2}$

```ad-example
collapse: close
title: Where
color: 200, 200, 200
X^2=f(x)
 L~$N\left(\mu, \sigma^{2}\right)$ 
 $f(x)=\frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2 \pi}} \cdot \exp \left(-\frac{1}{2} \frac{(x-\mu)^{2}}{\sigma^{2}}\right)$ 

f: degree of freedom  
f=n-1 when $s^{2}=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} \varepsilon_{j}^{2}$ 
```
